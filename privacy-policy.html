Privacy Policy

At Fables Fantasy, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, utilize, and protect your personal information when you access or make use of our services.

1. Information We Gather

We gather information that you willingly provide to us, such as when you create an account, participate in a contest, or reach out to our support team. This information may encompass:

  • Personal information (name, email address, etc.)
  • Account credentials (username, password, etc.)
  • Payment and billing details (for contest entries)

2. How We Utilize Your Information

Your information is utilized for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account and services
  • To handle payments and contest entries
  • To communicate with you about updates, offers, and assistance
  • To enhance our platform and services

3. Data Protection

We apply industry-standard security practices to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, lease, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared with reputable service providers who help us deliver our services, such as payment processors, provided they adhere to confidentiality agreements.
